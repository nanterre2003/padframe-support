Pad Frame

Turn your old iPad into a beautiful digital photo frame.

How to use

Open the app, tap the screen to open Settings, select your photo album, and plug in your iPad. Your photos will display automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open Settings?
Simply tap anywhere on the screen.

How do I select a specific album?
Tap the screen to open Settings, then tap the Album row to choose from your photo library.

Why does the screen turn off?
Make sure your iPad is plugged in while using Pad Frame. The screen stays on automatically when charging.

Which iPads are supported?
Pad Frame supports all iPads running iOS 13 or later.
