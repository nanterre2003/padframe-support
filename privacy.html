Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Overview

Pad Frame ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Pad Frame does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photos displayed in the app are accessed locally from your device's photo library and are never uploaded or shared.

Photo Library Access

Pad Frame requires access to your photo library solely to display your photos as a slideshow. We do not access, copy, or transmit your photos to any server.

Data Storage

App settings (such as transition effects and display preferences) are stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Pad Frame does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
